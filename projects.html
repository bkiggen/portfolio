Fake Wikipedia

Fake Wikipedia was our big bootstrap challenge. We were asked to combine all our knowledge of bootstrap, especially some of the grid mechanics, to (as faithfully as we could pull off) recreate the homepage of wikipedia. While we didn't get it dead on, it turned out a lot better looking that I thought it was going to half-way though building it!

Technologies Used:

  • CSS
  • Bootstrap
